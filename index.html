HRVey

An HRV analysis tool for 10-second ECG PDFs.

🚧 In development
  1. Load a scanned 10-second ECG PDF
  2. Level the scan and calibrate the time scale
  3. Mark each R-peak
  4. Read RR intervals, heart rate, RMSSD & SDNN — and export

Runs entirely in your browser. Your ECG files never leave your device.
